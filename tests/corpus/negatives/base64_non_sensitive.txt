Base64-shaped strings that do not decode to sensitive data

Each string below is a valid base64 token (matches the alphabet,
correct length) but decodes to non-sensitive content or binary
garbage. The scanner should not report SSN, credit card, or other
sensitive-data findings from these tokens.

encoded greeting: SGVsbG8gV29ybGQh
encoded lorem: TG9yZW0gaXBzdW0gZG9sb3Igc2l0IGFtZXQ=
binary blob (all A = all zeros): AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA
